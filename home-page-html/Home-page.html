Adopt a Pet

By adopting instead of buying an animal, you will save two lives-the pet you adopt and a homeless animal that can be rescued because of space you make available.

Animal Welfare

If an animal’s life is in immediate distress as a result of cruelty or neglect, please contact the police at 911. To report animal cruelty or neglect, please please call 1-833-9ANIMAL
(1-833-926-4625).

Statistics

Each year, approximately 4.1 million shelter animals are adopted. However, it's estimated that more than 1 million adoptable dogs and cats are euthanized due to a lack of welcoming homes.
